How Chinese Zodiac Compatibility Works

Traditional Korean Saju astrology measures compatibility through five core relationships between Earthly Branches: Six Harmonies (Yukhap, 六合) are the perfect-match pairs created by yin-yang complementarity; Three Harmonies (Samhap, 三合) are triplets that combine into one element; Six Clashes (Chung, 沖) sit directly opposite on the wheel and create tension; Six Harms (Hae, 害) hide friction under surface harmony. The remaining pairs are neutral, where outcomes depend on individual effort.
