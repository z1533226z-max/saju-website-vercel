How Saju Compatibility Works

Saju (사주, Four Pillars of Destiny) is a centuries-old Korean astrology system. While Chinese zodiac compatibility considers only the year-branch sign, a full Saju reading analyzes the year, month, day, and hour pillars together — eight characters in total.

Even at the year-branch level, five key relationships emerge: Six Harmonies (Yukhap) for perfect pairs, Three Harmonies (Samhap) for triple combinations into one element, Six Clashes (Chung) for direct opposition, and Six Harms (Hae) for hidden friction. The remaining pairings are neutral, where outcomes depend almost entirely on individual effort.
